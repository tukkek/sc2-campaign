Instructions:
  1. Start by clicking a free territory on the bottom row.
  2. Each turn, attack a hostile area next to one of yours.
  3. Play as any race you want. Challenge: use only your starting area or currently attacking area's race.
  4. The map and AIs (including the owner) for each battle are shown by hovering the mouse over it.
  5. If you win, click the target area to capture it. If you lose, click your own area to lose it instead.
  6. The first time you capture a territory, you earn credits. Use them to reduce an AI's power ($1 = -10%) or to hire allied AI once ($1 = Very Easy AI, $2 = Easy)...
  7. You can gamble your credits too! Give five dollars to Activision every time you do this.
  8. You win by capturing a territory on the top of the map or by reaching $100 in the bank. Challenge: double or triple those goals or give yourself a time limit.
