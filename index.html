Instructions
  1. Start by clicking a territory on the bottom row for free. (Challenge: start higher on the map).
  2. Each turn, attack a hostile area next to any of your owned territories.
  3. Play as any race you want. (Challenge: use only the race from your starting area or area you're attacking from).
  4. Play by setting up custom melee matches (or play a ladder match, if teams are even). Set-up as shown when hovering the mouse over the target area.
  5. If you win, click the target area to capture it. If you lose, click your own area to lose control over it instead.
  6. You earn credits when capturing an area for the first time. Use them to weaken an AI ($1 = -10%) or to hire an AI ally once ($1 = Very Easy, $2 = Easy)...
  7. You can gamble your credits! Give five dollars to Activision every time you do this, to feel pride and accomplishment.
  8. You win by capturing a territory on the top row or by having $50 in the bank. (Challenge: double or triple those goals or give yourself a time limit).
  9. Reload this page to get a new campaign.
